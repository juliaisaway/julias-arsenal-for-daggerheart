# Kapu, the Bearer

He eases the burden of fatigue… and chooses who will carry it next.

## Features

### Witness (1)

Whenever an ally marks 1 or more Hit Points, **place 1 token** on this card.

### Drain the Burden

**Spend 3 tokens** to have all allies within Very Close range clear 1 Stress.

### Transfer the Burden

**Spend 3 tokens** to have all adversaries within Very Close range mark 1 Stress.
