# Fionna MacTine

A pyromancer sorcerer whose temper ignites flames she cannot always control.

## Features

### Channel

Whenever this ally gains the spotlight, **place 1 token** on this card.

### Fireball

**Spend 6 tokens** to have _Fionna_ launch a sphere of fire at a target within Very Far range. The target and all creatures within Very Close range of it must make a Reaction Roll (13). Targets that fail take **3d20+5** magic damage. Targets that succeed take half damage.
