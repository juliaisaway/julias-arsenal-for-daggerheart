# Tamir Paracelion

An eccentric alchemist who claims to be perfecting “the ultimate formula of controlled combustion.”

## Features

### Inspired

Whenever an ally rolls with Hope on their Duality Dice, **place 1 token** on this card.

### Alchemical Bomb

**Spend 3 tokens** to have _Tamir_ throw an explosive at a target within Far range, dealing **1d20+3** physical damage.

### Refreshing Tonic

**Spend 3 tokens** to have _Tamir_ clear 1 Hit Point from an ally within Melee range.
