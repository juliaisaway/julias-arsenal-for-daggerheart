# Yakov Kargan

He bends the earth to his will, making his allies harder to bring down.

## Features

### Pressured

Whenever an ally rolls with Fear on their Duality Dice, **place 1 token** on this card.

### Stone Skin

**Spend 2 tokens** to choose an ally within Close range and increase their damage thresholds by +2. Only one ally can benefit from this ability at a time. You may spend 2 tokens again to choose a new target.
