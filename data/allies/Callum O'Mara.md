# Callum O'Mara

A humble farmer, devoted to his land and to those who depend on it.

## Features

### Triumph

Whenever an adversary is defeated, **place 1 token** on this card.

### Victory Song

**Spend 1 token** for an ally within Very Close range to gain 1 Hope.

### Harvest Song

**Spend 3 tokens** and choose two allies within Close range. They may immediately initiate a Tag Team Roll without spending Hope. This ability can only be used once per rest.
