# Karl Shatterbeard

A veteran of the slopes, he believes a good shot solves what words cannot.

## Features

### Opportunist

Whenever an ally fails an attack roll, **place 1 token** on this card.

### Hunter’s Lasso

**Spend 3 tokens** to make a target within Very Close range _Restrained_ and _Vulnerable_ temporarily.

### Sweeping Shot

**Spend 2 tokens** to have Karl fire in a cone. Roll 3d6 and distribute the damage among all adversaries within Very Close range in front of him.
