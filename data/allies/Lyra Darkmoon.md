# Lyra Darkmoon

Heir to a mystical lineage, she interprets fate through the cards of those who cross her path.

## Features

### Foresee

Whenever an ally uses an Experience, **place 1 token** on this card.

### Card of Fortune

**Spend 1 token** for an ally to add **1d6** to a roll that uses an Experience.

### Card of Ruin

**Spend 3 tokens** to choose an adversary within Close range. The next successful attack against that adversary deals **2d8** additional damage.
