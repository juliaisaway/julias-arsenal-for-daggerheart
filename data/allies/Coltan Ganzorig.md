# Coltan Ganzorig

With calloused hands and a fiery spirit, he fights both to survive and for those he loves.

## Features

### Triumph

Whenever an adversary is defeated, **place 1 token** on this card.

### Rebel Strike

**Spend 1 token** to have _Coltan_ deal **2d8+3** physical damage to an adversary within Melee range. If that adversary is _Restrained_, deal an additional 1d8 damage.

### Inspire the Rebellion

**Spend 3 tokens** for _Coltan_ to grant 1d4 to each ally within Very Close range. An ally can roll this die and add the result to an action roll, reaction roll, or damage roll. Unused dice are lost at the end of the scene.
