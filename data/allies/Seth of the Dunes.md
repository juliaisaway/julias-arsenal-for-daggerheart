# Seth of the Dunes

A wanderer of the arid lands, he has learned that survival is always the greatest victory.

## Features

### Pressured

Whenever an ally rolls with Fear on their Duality Dice, **place 1 token** on this card.

### Sand Step

**Spend 2 tokens** for _Seth_ to grant +2 Evasion to an ally within Close range until the next attack against them.
