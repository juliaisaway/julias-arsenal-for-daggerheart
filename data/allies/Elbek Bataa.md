# Elbek Bataa

With a sharp eye and few words, he turns mistakes into second chances.

## Features

### Prepared (2)

This card begins with **2 tokens** and cannot gain additional tokens until the next rest.

### Second Chance

When an ally within Close range fails a roll, **spend 1 token** for _Elbek_ to allow them to reroll one of their Duality Dice.
