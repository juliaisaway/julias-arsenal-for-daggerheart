# Iruanê Anayru

She knows every stone, every fungus, and every crawling thing that dwells within the depths of the caverns.

## Features

### Track

Whenever an ally deals damage to an adversary, **place 1 token** on this card.

### Spore Veil

**Spend 3 tokens** to make an ally within Close range _Hidden_ temporarily.

### Exposed Trail

**Spend 3 tokens** to make an adversary within Close range _Vulnerable_ temporarily.

## Design notes

The name _Iruanê Anayru_ draws inspiration from the cultures and languages of Indigenous peoples of the Amazon Rainforest in Brazil.
