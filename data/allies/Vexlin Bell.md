# Vexlin Bell

A small student who used to fall asleep in class... until she realized it could be her greatest strength.

## Features

### Channel

Whenever this ally gains the spotlight, **place 1 token** on this card.

### Drowsiness

**Spend 2 tokens** to have _Vexlin_ make an adversary within Very Close range [_Asleep_](/mechanics/conditions/).

### Mass Sleep

**Spend 6 tokens** to have _Vexlin_ make 1d4 adversaries within Far range [_Asleep_](/mechanics/conditions/).
