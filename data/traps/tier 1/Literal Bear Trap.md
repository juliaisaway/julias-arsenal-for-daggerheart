# Literal Bear Trap

A crude metal contraption lies hidden on the ground, its jaws slightly ajar. It looks poorly made… almost suspiciously so.

## Purpose

Introduce an unexpected threat, disrupt the scene, subvert player expectations.

## Features

### Shot

#### Trigger

When a PC interacts with or steps onto the trap

#### Effect

Instead of snapping shut, the trap springs open with a loud clank. A Bear (Tier 1 Bruiser) suddenly appears within Close range of the trap, immediately aware of the nearest creature. The bear acts immediately after being spawned.

## Design notes

I read this joke years ago on Reddit. It's a playful subversion of expectations. What appears to be a simple trap instead introduces a threat to the players.
