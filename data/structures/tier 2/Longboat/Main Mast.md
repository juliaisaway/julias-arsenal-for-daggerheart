# Longboat Main Mast

## Features

### Broken Rigging - Passive

When the Main Mast is Destroyed, all PCs must mark a Stress and the Longboat can no longer advance its countdown through the Main Mast.

### Hold Course - Action

A PC within the Main Mast can try to keep the Longboat on course through the storm. They make an Instinct Roll. On a success, advance the Longboat’s countdown. On a critical success, advance the countdown twice.
