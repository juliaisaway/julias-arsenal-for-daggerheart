# Longboat Stern

## Features

### Fatal Breach - Passive

When the Stern is Destroyed, the Longboat is destroyed.

### Steady the Rudder - Action

If there are no adversaries within the Stern, a PC within the Stern can carefully guide the Longboat through the storm. They make a Finesse Roll. On a success, advance the Longboat’s countdown. On a critical success, advance the countdown twice.

### Defend the Stern - Reaction

Whenever an adversary is defeated within the Stern, advance the Longboat’s countdown.
