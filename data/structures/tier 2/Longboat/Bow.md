# Longboat Bow

## Features

### Fatal Breach - Passive

When the Bow is Destroyed, the Longboat is destroyed.

### Bail Water - Action

A PC within the Bow can try to remove water from the flooded hull. They make an Agility Roll. On a success, advance the Longboat’s countdown. On a critical success, advance the countdown twice.
