# Longboat Deck

## Features

### Collapsing Deck - Passive

When the Deck is Destroyed, broken cargo and debris litter the deck. Whenever a PC ends their Spotlight within the Deck, they must mark a Stress.

### Secure Cargo - Action

A PC within the Deck can secure loose cargo before it crashes across the ship. They make a Strength Roll. On a success, advance the Longboat’s countdown. On a critical success, advance the countdown twice.
