# Ikeri Torso

## Features

### Fatal - Passive

When the Torso is Destroyed, Ikeri is defeated.

### Shake Off - Action

Spend a Fear to throw off foes. All creatures on Ikeri must make a Strength Reaction Roll. Targets who fail fall to the ground and must mark a HP. Targets who succeed must mark a Stress.
