# Canin

Canin are humanoid canines, bearing fur, muzzles, and the keen senses of their animal kin.

## Features

### Trailfinder

You have advantage on Instinct Rolls that involve tracking a person, creature, or trail.

### Pack Coordination

Once per session, when you participate in a Tag Team Roll, you can reroll your Duality Dice.

## Design notes

A player of mine wants to play a _Katari_-equivalent of a Dog, so I homebrewed this version, trying to encapsulate all the traits a dog-like companion would have.
