# Vestari

Vestari are blue-skinned humanoids marked by the echoes of countless past lives.

## Features

### Ancestral Recall

Fragments of past lives guide your actions. Spend a Hope to gain a +2 bonus on your next roll.

### Borrowed Vitality

You draw on past lives at a cost. Whenever you would spend a Hope, you can instead mark a Hit Point.

## Design notes

The _Samsaran_ has always been my favorite ancestry in _Pathfinder_. Its themes of identity and continuity resonated with me on a personal level, so adapting it to Daggerheart felt especially meaningful.
