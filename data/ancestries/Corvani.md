# Corvani

Corvani are avian humanoids with feathered bodies, sharp beaks, and powerful wings.

## Features

### Take Flight

You can fly. While flying, you can mark a Stress to move to a location within Far range.

### Razor Beak

You can make attacks against a target within Close range using your beak, treating it as a Finesse weapon that deals 1d8 physical damage using your Proficiency.

## Design notes

I started worldbuild a decade ago, using _Pathfinder_ system and my campaign had a lot of Tengus. SO I tried to homebrew an equivalent for Daggerheart.
