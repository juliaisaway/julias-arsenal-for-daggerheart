# Gravemaw Juggernaut

A massive, animate boulder with a crude, grinning face. It hurls itself across the battlefield with reckless force, each impact cracking its own unstable core.

## Motives & Tactics

Crush targets through repeated impacts, build momentum, ignore self-preservation

## Features

### Compressed Mass - Passive

The Gravemaw Juggernaut has resistance to physical damage. Damage dealt to the Juggernaut does not reduce its Stress.

### Runaway Momentum - Passive

Each time the Juggernaut moves or is forced to move, it must mark a Stress.

### Crushing Impact - Action

Make a standard attack. On a success, if the Juggernaut moved before this attack, increase the damage by 1d10.

### Unstable Core - Passive

When the Juggernaut marks its last Stress, it explodes and is defeated. Each creature within Close range must make an Agility Reaction Roll (16) or take 2d10 physical damage.

## Design notes

When I played *Clair Obscur: Expedition 33*, I encounter a boss called *Grosse Tete* with a unique and fun mechanic, so I tried to replicate here.
