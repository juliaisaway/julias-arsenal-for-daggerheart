# Minor Water Elemental

A living surge of seawater formed amidst the storm.

## Motives & Tactics

Disperse, destabilize, overwhelm

## Features

### Rushing Water - Action

Mark a Stress to attack a target within Very Close range. On a success, deal 2d4+2 physical damage and the target becomes Vulnerable until the end of their next action.

### Surging Tide - Action

Spend a Fear to make an attack against all targets within Very Close range. Targets the Elemental succeeds against are knocked back to Close range.

## Design notes

This adversary was inspired by the _Greater Water Elemental_, from the [_Daggerheart System Reference Document_](https://www.daggerheart.com/srd/ "Daggerheart System Reference Document"), adapted into a smaller support-focused creature. Its mechanics were simplified to emphasize repositioning, vulnerability, and environmental pressure rather than raw damage output.
