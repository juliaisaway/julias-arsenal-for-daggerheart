# Crimson Rite Martyr

A heavily armored zealot who offers their own flesh and suffering to protect the Crimson Rite. Martyrs hurl themselves into battle without hesitation, holding enemies in place while the ritual reaches its climax.

## Motives & Tactics

Protect the Cult, lock down enemies, overwhelm nearby targets

## Features

### Hymn of Violence - Passive

You must spend a Fear to spotlight the Martyr. While spotlighted, they can make their standard attack against all targets within range.

### Blood Offering - Action

Mark a Stress to make a standard attack against a single target with advantage. If they mark 3 or more HP, you gain a Fear.

### Stand Before the Rite - Reaction

If a PC within Melee range attempts to leave that range, they must make a Strength Reaction Roll. On a failure, they become Restrained temporarily.

### Final Offering - Reaction

When the Martyr is defeated, they make an attack against all targets within Melee range. On a success, the target takes 2d6+6 physical damage.

### Momentum - Reaction

When the Martyr makes a successful attack against a PC, you gain a Fear.
