# Crimson Rite Conduit

A ritual conduit who sustains the cult through blood rites, shared suffering, and unwavering devotion.

## Motives & Tactics

Sustain allied cultists, exploit targets, preserve the Cult at any cost

## Features

### Communion of Pain - Action

Spend a Fear to have an ally within Very Close range clear 1d4 HP. You can also mark a Stress to have them clear a Stress.

### Feed the Spiral - Action

Make a standard attack against a target. On a success, if they are [_Spiraling_](/mechanics/conditions/), they must mark a Stress.

### Final Benediction - Reaction

When the Conduit is defeated, an ally within Close range clears 1 HP.
