# Shadowbolt Skirmisher

A crossbow ambusher who strikes from cover, then vanishes before retaliation.

## Motives & Tactics

Isolate targets, fire from shadows, free when pressured

## Features

### Lucky - Passive

The Skirmisher can reroll a 1 on an action roll

### Unseen Strike - Passive

If the Skirmisher is Hidden when they make a successful standard attack against a target, it deals 2d8+3 physicalm damage instead of their standard damage.

### Maintain Distance - Passive

After making a standard attack, the Skirmisher can move anywhere within Far range.

### Cloaked - Action

Mark a Stress to have the Skirmisher become Hidden until its next attack. Attacks made while Hidden from this feature have advantage.
