# Crimson Rite Acolyte

A ritual conduit who sustains the cult through blood rites, shared suffering, and unwavering devotion.

## Motives & Tactics

Sustain allied cultists, exploit targets, preserve the Cult at any cost

## Features

### Minion (7) - Passive

The Acolyte is defeated when they take any damage. For every 7 damage a PC deals to the Acolyte, defeat an additional Minion within range the attack would succeed against.

### Group Attack - Action

Spend a Fear to choose a target and spotlight all Crimson Rite Acolyte within Close range of them. Those Minions move into Melee range of the target and make one shared attack roll. On a success, they deal 5 physical damage each. Combine this damage.
