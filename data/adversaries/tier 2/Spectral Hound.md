# Spectral Hound

A ghostly hunting hound bound to an ancient battlefield, phasing in and out of reality as it tracks its prey.

## Motives & Tactics

Stalk silently, isolate prey, strike from beyond the veil

## Features

### Ghost - Passive

The Hound has resistance to physical damage. Mark a Stress to move up to Close range through solid objects.

### Phasing Stalker - Passive

While the Hound is not in direct line of sight of any PC, it is considered Hidden.

### Spectral Ambush - Passive

While Hidden, mark a Stress to emerge into Melee range of a target within Very Close range. The target must succeed on an Agility or Instinct Reaction Roll (15) or take 2d8 magic damage and become temporarily Restrained.

### Ghostly Roar - Action

Spend a Fear to roar, causing a spectral energy to propagate. All targets within Close range must succeed on an Presence Reaction Roll (14) or take 2d8 magic damage and become Vulnerable temporarily.

## Design notes

This is my take on the *Stonewraith* from the [*Daggerheart System Reference Document*](https://www.daggerheart.com/srd/), but changed to be a Spectral unit, alongside *Spectral Guardian*, *Spectral Captain* and *Spectral Archer*.
