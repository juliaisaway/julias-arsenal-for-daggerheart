# Welcome to Julia's Arsenal

This is a collection of Adversaries, Environments and Traps that I used in my tabletop games. Most of it I tested with my players in a bi-weekly schedule.

Check the sidebar and click an item to see the details.

Feel free to give any feedback to [hello@eusoujulia.com.br](mailto:hello@eusoujulia.com.br "Send me an e-mail") or on my [GitHub repository](https://github.com/juliaisaway/julias-arsenal-for-daggerheart "The GitHub repository for this project").
